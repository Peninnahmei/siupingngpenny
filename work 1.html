PENNY, SIU PING, NG (She/Her) - Visual Designer & Founder of beatitudes attitudes

Based in Toronto, Canada. PENNY, SIU PING, NG (She/Her) is a freelance visual designer dedicated to intergrade art and graphic design in branding and editorial design. Background in Fashion Design and Fine Art, Penny is currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Creative Arts at Yorkville University in Toronto, Canada.

She is passionate about graphic design (AI, PS, 3D Sampler, 3D Stager), creative coding (processing 4), and web design and coding (Figma & Atom) which allow her to build an immersive visual experience.

For past working experience, Penny worked as a garment technologist at Burberry London Company and has been working for many years in the fashion field. She is skilled and experienced in 3D CLO and Gerber accumark for 2D and 3D paper pattern drafting and digitalizing.
